Grilled Cheese

How to Make Grilled Cheese

This page is going to teach you how to make a delicious meal without breaking the bank.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • American Cheese Sliced
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Use a knife to spread butter on one side of two slices of bread
  2. Put butter side down on hot pan
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add last piece of bread butter side up
  5. Flip grilled cheese once side starts to brown
  6. Once both sides are crisp, remove from stove and enjoy
